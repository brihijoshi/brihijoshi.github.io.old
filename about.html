A B O U T
"A little dust particle in the Universe, who went on to write her destiny. Or did she?"

Hey there! I am Brihi (pronounced Bree-hee). If you wondered, "that sounds strange", it means a bridge that connects two worlds. As for my world, it comprises of my Guruji and parents. Though I don't have a sibling, I'm pretty sure I am enough to handle! Click here for the website my parents made for me when I was two!

Born in Lucknow, I have lived in places like Pithoragarh, Dehradun, Guwahati (seven years!) and finally moved to Delhi in 2014.
Sarala Birla Gyan Jyoti, my school in Guwahati, helped me shape most of who I am right now. A small place, they say. But definitely an influential one.
Delhi happened during those two long, long years of JEE preparation, when my sole outings were to school or to the coaching centre. Sanskriti (my school) was a breath of fresh air during those days.
And then came IIIT Delhi. The present year had its ups and downs, but IIIT has been the best experience so far.

I think I am one of those kinds who are reserved when you first meet them, but then when you get to know them, they are so much more.
